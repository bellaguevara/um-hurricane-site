Five National Championships

Miami Hurricanes football won five national championships in 1983, 1987, 1989, 1991, and 2001. The 2001 team is considered one of the greatest college football teams ever, with future NFL stars Ed Reed, Sean Taylor, and Andre Johnson.

The Turnover Chain

The Turnover Chain is one of the most famous traditions in college football. When a Miami defender forces a fumble or interception, they get to wear a giant gold chain on the sideline. The chain started in 2017 and has become a symbol of Miami football swagger.

The Hurricanes play home games at Hard Rock Stadium in Miami Gardens. On game day, thousands of fans show up early to tailgate in orange, green, and white.
