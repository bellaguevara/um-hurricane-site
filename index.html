Home of the Miami Hurricanes

The University of Miami Hurricanes are one of the most famous sports programs in college athletics. The Hurricanes play in the ACC conference and have 18 varsity sports teams. Miami is best known for football, with five national championships, but the school also has championship programs in baseball and basketball.

This website covers the sports teams at The U. Click the links above to learn more about Hurricanes football or the other sports Miami competes in.
