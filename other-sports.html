Baseball

Miami baseball has won four national championships in 1982, 1985, 1999, and 2001. The team plays at Alex Rodriguez Park on campus. Famous former players include Alex Rodriguez, Ryan Braun, and Pat Burrell.

Basketball

The Miami men's basketball team made the Final Four in 2023 for the first time in school history. The women's basketball team also had a great season that year, reaching the Elite Eight. Both teams play home games at the Watsco Center.

Other Teams

Miami also has strong programs in tennis, golf, soccer, rowing, track and field, and swimming. Rowing practices take place on Lake Osceola right on the UM campus. All together, the Hurricanes have 18 varsity sports teams that compete in the ACC conference.
